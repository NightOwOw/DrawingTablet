Welcome to Ciona's Cute Tablet!

  • Use the brush tool to draw on the canvas
  • Adjust brush size and opacity with the sliders
  • Choose colors from the color wheel
  • Press R to switch between brush and eraser
  • Press F to use the fill bucket
  • Press Ctrl+Z to undo and Ctrl+Y to redo
  • Manage your artwork with layers on the right panel
  • Press S to save your artwork
